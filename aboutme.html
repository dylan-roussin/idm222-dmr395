About Me

Hi! I'm Dylan Roussin and I'm pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Interactive Digital Media. I have experience working as a graphic designer and am very interested in learning about user interface and user experience design. I also love making things and am interested in learning about devlopment and programming.

As a graphic designer, I worked on a variety of projects from creating advertisments, motion graphics, and clothing designs. Working on graphics in a real world setting gave me insight into the process of working in a team, and learning to make changes based on client input.

In my free time, I like working on projects using Arduino, my digital art projects, websites, music, or any other creative project I find interesting. I love the process of making things, and all the ways in which these different creative processes can be combined.

In the future, I'd like to focus more of my time working on development and programming. I find that I really enjoy the small increments of progress that come from creating something new, and using the possibilities that programming allows means that I can create things I am interested in and passionate.
